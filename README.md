# QSR Industry Data

Open data and analysis for the U.S. quick service restaurant industry.

## About

This repository contains publicly available data, research notes, and analytical frameworks related to the quick service restaurant (QSR) industry in the United States.

Published by [QSR Pro](https://qsr.pro) - independent, data-driven coverage of the QSR industry.

## Coverage Areas

- **Franchise Cost Analysis**: Unit economics and investment requirements sourced from Franchise Disclosure Documents (FDDs) for 50+ QSR brands
- **Technology and Automation**: AI ordering systems, kitchen automation, and operational technology ROI analysis
- **Supply Chain and Commodities**: Beef, chicken, and agricultural commodity price tracking with impact analysis for QSR operators
- **Real Estate and Development**: New unit development trends, site selection data, and market saturation analysis
- **Financial Performance**: Revenue trends, same-store sales data, and margin analysis across major QSR chains

## Data Sources

All data is sourced from publicly available documents including:

- SEC filings (10-K, 10-Q, 8-K)
- Franchise Disclosure Documents (FDD Item 19 and Item 7)
- USDA Economic Research Service
- Bureau of Labor Statistics
- Company earnings calls and investor presentations
- National Restaurant Association reports
