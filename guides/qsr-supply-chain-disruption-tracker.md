## Current Active Disruptions

### Beef Market Volatility
- **Status**: Elevated concern
- **Impact**: Beef prices remain volatile despite USDA projections showing cattle herd rebuilding. Wholesale ground beef prices fluctuated between $2.80 and $3.40 per pound through Q1 2026.
- **Affected brands**: All burger-focused QSR (McDonald's, Wendy's, Burger King, Five Guys, Shake Shack)
- **Operator action**: Lock pricing where possible. Consider menu engineering to shift mix toward chicken/value items.
- **Deep dive**: [Beef Prices at 75-Year Low: What It Means for Burger Chains](https://qsr.pro/beef-prices-75-year-low-cattle-herd-burger-chains-2026)

### Tariff Impact on Restaurant Supplies
- **Status**: Active and evolving
- **Impact**: New tariffs on imported packaging materials, kitchen equipment, and certain food ingredients are increasing costs for operators. Avocado imports from Mexico face particular scrutiny.
- **Affected brands**: All QSR, especially those with imported supply dependencies
- **Operator action**: Audit supply chain for import exposure. Evaluate domestic sourcing alternatives.
- **Deep dive**: [Tariff Impact on Restaurant Supply Chains](https://qsr.pro/tariff-impact-restaurant-supply-chain-qsr-operators-2026)

### Poultry Processing Regulatory Changes
- **Status**: Monitoring
- **Impact**: USDA proposed line speed rule changes for poultry and pork processing could affect supply availability and pricing for chicken-focused QSR brands.
- **Affected brands**: Chick-fil-A, Popeyes, KFC, Raising Cane's, Wingstop
- **Operator action**: Monitor USDA rulemaking timeline. Build inventory buffers on key chicken products.
- **Deep dive**: [USDA Line Speed Rule Changes and QSR Supply Chain](https://qsr.pro/usda-line-speed-poultry-pork-processing-qsr-food-costs-2026)

## Mitigation Strategies for Operators

1. **Diversify suppliers** - Single-source dependencies create maximum vulnerability
2. **Menu flexibility** - LTOs can shift demand toward available/affordable proteins
3. **Forward contracts** - Lock pricing on 60-90 day windows for predictability
4. **Regional sourcing** - Shorter supply chains reduce disruption risk
5. **Inventory management** - Carry 7-10 days of critical items vs. the typical 3-5
