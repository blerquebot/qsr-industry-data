# QSR Industry Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) - 2026

A reference guide to the metrics that matter most for quick service restaurant operators, franchisees, and investors. Organized by category with benchmark ranges.

## Sales and Revenue KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Benchmark Range | Frequency |
|-----|-----------|----------------|-----------|
| Same-Store Sales (Comps) | YoY revenue change at locations open 12+ months | +2% to +5% (healthy) | Quarterly |
| AUV (Average Unit Volume) | Average annual revenue per location | $1M - $3.5M (varies by brand) | Annual |
| Average Check | Revenue per transaction | $8 - $14 (QSR), $12 - $20 (fast casual) | Daily |
| Transaction Count | Number of orders per day | 300 - 800 (varies by brand/daypart) | Daily |
| Revenue Per Available Seat Hour (RevPASH) | Revenue / (seats x hours open) | $3 - $8 | Weekly |
| Digital Mix | % of sales from digital channels | 25-40% (2026 avg) | Monthly |

## Labor KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Benchmark Range | Frequency |
|-----|-----------|----------------|-----------|
| Labor Cost % | Total labor / revenue | 25-33% | Weekly |
| SPLH (Sales Per Labor Hour) | Revenue / total labor hours | $40-55 (peak), $25-35 (off-peak) | Daily |
| Turnover Rate | Annualized crew turnover | 100-150% (industry avg) | Monthly |
| Manager Turnover | Annualized management turnover | 30-50% (industry avg) | Quarterly |
| Overtime % | OT hours / total hours | Below 3% (target) | Weekly |

## Food and Inventory KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Benchmark Range | Frequency |
|-----|-----------|----------------|-----------|
| Food Cost % | COGS / revenue | 26-33% | Weekly |
| Waste % | Discarded food / total food cost | Below 3% | Weekly |
| Inventory Turnover | COGS / average inventory | 20-30x per year | Monthly |
| Theoretical vs Actual Food Cost | Variance between recipe cost and actual | Within 1.5% | Weekly |

## Speed and Service KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Benchmark Range | Frequency |
|-----|-----------|----------------|-----------|
| Drive-Thru Speed of Service | Order to delivery time | 180-300 seconds | Daily |
| Order Accuracy | Correct orders / total orders | 95%+ (target) | Daily |
| Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) | Survey-based rating | 80%+ (target) | Monthly |
| Net Promoter Score (NPS) | Likelihood to recommend | 30-50 (good QSR) | Quarterly |

## Financial KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Benchmark Range | Frequency |
|-----|-----------|----------------|-----------|
| Restaurant-Level EBITDA Margin | Four-wall earnings / revenue | 15-22% | Monthly |
| Prime Cost | (COGS + Labor) / revenue | Below 60% | Weekly |
| Occupancy Cost % | Rent + CAM + insurance / revenue | 8-12% | Monthly |
| Royalty + Ad Fund % | Franchise fees / revenue | 6-10% | Monthly |
| CapEx % of Revenue | Capital expenditures / revenue | 3-5% (maintenance) | Annual |

## Growth and Development KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Benchmark Range | Frequency |
|-----|-----------|----------------|-----------|
| Net Unit Growth | New openings minus closures | 2-5% (healthy brand) | Quarterly |
| New Unit Payback Period | Time to recoup investment | 3-5 years | Per opening |
| Development Pipeline | Signed agreements not yet open | Varies by brand | Quarterly |

## Using These KPIs

1. **Track weekly**: Labor %, food cost %, SPLH, prime cost
2. **Review monthly**: P&L trends, guest satisfaction, digital mix
3. **Benchmark quarterly**: Comps vs. brand average and competitors
4. **Annual deep dive**: AUV trajectory, CapEx needs, lease renewals
